BMI, or Body Mass Index, is like a trusty yardstick in the world of health and fitness. It's a simple math equation where you divide your weight by the square of your height. The result falls into categories classified in the image above. This can give you a ballpark idea of how your weight stacks up against your height. Note: BMI is handy for a quick health check, but it doesn't replace the need to visit your doctor for diagnosis. It is merely like the first page of your health story, not the whole book.
